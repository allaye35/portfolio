Academic Web Scraping Project

University of Nice Sophia Antipolis, France

Year of Completion: September 2023 to January 2024

As part of my Master's in MIAGE, I developed a web scraping project targeting LinkedIn to extract information about former MIAGE students from the University of Nice. The goal was to collect, clean, and analyze data concerning their academic and professional paths, as well as the experiences and characteristics of the companies where they worked.

This project allowed me to apply advanced programming techniques in Python, utilizing Flask as a backend framework and Angular for the user interface development. The extracted data were cleaned and structured before being stored in a MySQL database, enabling detailed statistical analyses on the professional and academic trajectories of the MIAGE graduates.

The outcomes of this project provide a valuable overview of the career trends of former MIAGE students, contributing to a better understanding of professional opportunities and competencies developed during the course.
